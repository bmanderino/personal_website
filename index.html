Cedar Rapids Chamber of Commerce

As the Manager of Creative Services for WebLink International, I am simultaneously a creative director, web designer, usability specialist and project manager. WebLink's primary focus is in developing websites for Chambers of Commerce, nationwide. My role is to meet with clients about their business goals and design needs, and translate that into a creative strategy. Working with my team of designers, I produce concepts, graphic elements and Flash animations which we then turn into standards-based sites. I also work closely with our back-end application developers to ensure the designs meet the technical requirements of our proprietary software.

Masterlock/Marvel Comics

Masterlock, the manufacturer of security devices, teamed up with Marvel Comics to create a promotional website for their tie-in with the "Incredible Hulk" movie (the Ang Lee / Eric Bana version). On the site, the user could enter codes found on marked Masterlock products to win prizes. They could also provide dialogue to a classic Hulk comic for a chance to win a trip to the Marvel Comics office in New York. Cramer-Krasselt, the agency of record, contracted me to develop the website. As the creative had already been approved, my role was to produce the code and graphics–along with the interactive dialogue editing feature–and then oversee the testing phase prior to launch.

Atomic Moguls/Premier League Facebook App

Fantasy Moguls developed a soccer-based sports game for Facebook, wherein users would pick the winners of the Premier League football matches.

Fantasy Moguls approached me to create a visually appealing interface design for the application. I developed a number of options for them to choose from, eventually paring down to the final version. The graphic assets were then turned over to the development team for production of the application.

Lind-Waldock

Lind-Waldock, a leader in online Futures trading, needed an updated look to their website and turned to Azavar Technologies to help realize their goals.

During the beginning phases of this project, I had an active role in developing the information architecture of the site. I developed wireframes for the various sections of the site and wrote a comprehensive creative brief, detailing various aspects of the design and functionality.

When the project moved into the production phase, I was a key member of the development of the site: I wrote the HTML and created the initial stylesheets for the templates that would make up the website and then helped implement those templates into the existing framework of the site.

Springfield Chamber of Commerce
